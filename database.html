Database Management

SQL databases are foundational for data storage and management in web and enterprise applications.
PostgreSQL stands out in the SQL database landscape due to its advanced features, extensibility, and excellent standards compliance.
It is a powerful, open-source RDBMS that handles a wide range of use cases, from complex analytics and geospatial applications to transactional systems and web apps.
The combination of reliability, flexibility, and support for complex data types makes PostgreSQL a top choice for developers looking to build robust, scalable applications.
